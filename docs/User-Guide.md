# ElateTrips — Quick Guide

**Plan the trip *and* the celebration — all in one place.** 🎉

ElateTrips helps you book a celebration getaway end to end: the stay, the
decorations, the gifts, the experiences and the travel — together, in one simple
flow.

---

## What you can do

- 🎂 **Celebrate anything** — birthdays, anniversaries, honeymoons, weddings,
  proposals, bachelor parties… or just **escape** with wellness, adventure,
  leisure or nature.
- 🏨 **Book the stay** and add **celebration setups** — decor, candlelight
  dinners, photoshoots, live music, spa, and more.
- 🤫 **Plan a surprise** — partner hotels can set up your room *before* the guest
  of honour arrives, kept hidden from your co-traveller.
- 🚕 **Sort the travel** — bring your own, or book a cab with pickup search and a
  fare estimate.
- 🎁 **Add gifts & essentials** — flowers, cakes, hampers and travel health kits,
  all in one cart.

---

## How it works — 4 easy steps

1. **Plan** — choose your destination, dates, travellers and what you're celebrating.
2. **Hotels** — pick a stay, a room, and the celebration touches you want.
3. **Shopping** *(optional)* — add surprise gifts or a travel medical kit.
4. **Review** — sign in with a quick one-time code, confirm details, and you're set.

A progress bar shows where you are, and you can go back any time — nothing is lost.

## Quick answers

- **Must I book a celebration package?** No — book just the trip, or add as much
  as you like.
- **Can I plan a surprise?** Yes — the room can be set up before your guest arrives.
- **Can I add gifts to my trip?** Yes — gifts and trip check out together.
- **Run a hotel or offer event services?** Tap **"Partner with us"** to join — free,
  no commitment.
